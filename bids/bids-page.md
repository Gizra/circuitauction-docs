# Bids Page

The Bids page provides a high-level summary of all bidding activity for the current sale.

## Summary Statistics

At the top of the page, you'll see bid summary statistics, including:

- Items count in sale, on the bid server, and in the search index
- Number of items with bids, including ratio and totals of bids, bidders, commissions, and financial totals
