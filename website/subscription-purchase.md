# Purchase Subscription

**Selector:** `#subscription-purchase` &nbsp;•&nbsp; *one per page*

## What it does

Displays the available subscription plans as a grid (ordered by price), each with its name, price and description. The bidder picks a plan, confirms in a summary modal, and is taken to billing to complete the purchase. Visitors who aren't logged in are prompted to sign in first.

## How to add it

```html
<div id="subscription-purchase" class="circuit-user-ui"></div>
```

## Options

None.
