# My Bids

**Selector:** `#my-bids` &nbsp;•&nbsp; *one per page* &nbsp;•&nbsp; **login required**

## What it does

The bidder's dashboard for their bids and credit, split into two tabs:

- **Active Sales** — ongoing bids grouped per sale, with filters (all / highest / not highest) and a toggle between a summary table and a detailed view.
- **Past Sales** — pick a closed sale from a dropdown and review your bids, filtered by all / won / lost.

Both tabs also surface the bidder's **available credit** and their **maximum bid limit**.
