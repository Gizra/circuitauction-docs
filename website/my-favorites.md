# My Favorites

**Selector:** `#my-favorites` &nbsp;•&nbsp; *one per page* &nbsp;•&nbsp; **login required**

## What it does

Lists the items the bidder has favorited (via the [Favorite Button](website/favorite-button.md) or item pages). It offers a sale filter, search (by lot, title or description), sorting (lot or price, ascending/descending), an items‑per‑page selector (12/24/36/48) and a summary/detailed view toggle. Bidders can also pick up to four items and open a side‑by‑side **comparison** modal.

